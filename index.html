Shipping Policy

At iOS Mobile Hub, we strive to process and ship your orders as quickly as possible. Please read our shipping policy carefully to understand our processes and timelines.

Shipping Areas

We currently ship to all major cities across Pakistan. For remote areas, additional shipping charges may apply.

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. During sale periods or holidays, processing may take slightly longer.

Shipping Methods & Time

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days - Rs. 150
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days - Rs. 300
  • Free Shipping: Available on orders over Rs. 2000 (Standard shipping only)

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS to monitor your package's journey.

Shipping Restrictions

We do not ship to P.O. boxes. A physical address is required for all deliveries.

International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within Pakistan. International shipping options will be available soon.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our shipping policy, please contact us at info@iosmobilehub.com or call +92 300 1234567.

Return & Refund Policy

At iOS Mobile Hub, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Please review our return and refund policy below.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

Return Timeframe

You have 7 calendar days to return an item from the date you received it.

Non-returnable Items

  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable software products
  • Some health and personal care items
  • Opened accessories (unless defective)

Return Process

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for return. We will guide you through the process.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 7-10 business days.

Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange it for the same item, contact us at info@iosmobilehub.com.

Shipping Costs for Returns

You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash on delivery, bank transfers, and soon we'll be integrating EasyPaisa and JazzCash for online payments.

How long does delivery take?
Delivery times vary by location. In major cities, standard delivery takes 3-5 business days, while express delivery takes 1-2 business days.

Do you offer international shipping?
Currently, we only ship within Pakistan. We're working on expanding our services to include international shipping in the future.

Returns & Warranty

What is your return policy?
We offer a 7-day return policy for unused items in original packaging. Please see our detailed Return Policy for more information.

Are your products original?
Yes, we guarantee that all our products are 100% original and come with manufacturer warranties where applicable.

Do you offer warranty on products?
Yes, most of our products come with manufacturer warranties. The warranty period varies by product, so please check individual product pages for details.

Shipping & Tracking

How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS that you can use to monitor your package's delivery status.

What should I do if I receive a damaged product?
If you receive a damaged product, please contact our customer service within 24 hours of delivery with photos of the damaged item, and we will arrange for a replacement or refund.
